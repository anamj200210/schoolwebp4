school life

laboratories

SCIENCE LAB

The Science laboratory experiences provide opportunities for students to interact directly with the material or with data drawn, using the tools, data collection techniques, models and theories of science. Our labs are well equipped with the latest apparatus Students in a hands-on science programme will remember the subject matter better, feel a sense of accomplishment when the task is completed, and be able to transfer that experience easier to other learning situations. Science education aims to provide scientific literacy for all as part of a liberal education and to prepare students for further study, and research work.

GERMAN LAB

We at Lotus Valley believe in giving a Global Exposure to our students & make them reach the targets in their life. Therefore, we take great pride in announcing that we are currently working on a joint venture with Max Mueller Bhavan/Goethe Institut, Delhi on a project called "Partner Schulen". Under this project the students are given a vast exposure to the German language, culture & tradition with the help of a German Lab, equipped with over 500 books, A. V. aids and an Exchange Program with The Julius-Echter-Gymnasium(School), Elsenfeld. Activities are conducted in this programme such as Inter School German Art Competition, Youth Camps in India & in Germany.

MATHS LAB

To facilitate directed discovery, learning and develop analytical thinking, we have provided the students with a well -equipped Maths lab. Our highly qualified and experienced Maths faculty imparts a strong logical orientation to the entire learning experience.




Cafeteria


Good nutrition is important for a focused mind and eating healthy, nutritional meal, keeps the child's mind sharp and alert. Keeping this in mind our innovatively decorated cafeteria - "Hunger Cure" serves healthy and nutritious snacks in hygienic and healthy surroundings. We, at parental request, serve regular lunches, keeping in mind the proper dietary requirements for the growing child. The menu of the meal served to the students is not only high in nutritional value but also caters to the tastes of our children. Our cafeteria in the A Block can seat around 100 students and the cafeteria in the C Block can seat around 500 students.

Infirmary

Our health unit is staffed by a full -time doctor & nurses who monitor the general physical development of our children and provide immediate first-aid treatment if needed. Arrangements exist for oxygen cylinder and nebulizer."list of cbse schools in greater noidaCheck ups for dental, eyesight, weight and height management are conducted on regular basis. This benefit's in identifying any problem at the very initial stage. The school has a tie-up with Indraprastha Apollo Hospital, Sarita Vihar, in case of an untoward requirement of advanced medical facilities . We have a qualified full time in-house doctor from the Hospital.
